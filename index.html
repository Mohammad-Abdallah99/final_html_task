Shawarman restaurant

Shawarman restaurant in Palestine, founded by chef Ahmad Al-Tamimi in 2010, is known for its delicious chicken and lamb shawarma, prepared with unique traditional flavors. Shawarma with various sauces is among the most popular dishes, and the restaurant is famous for its quality ingredients and excellent customer service, making it a favorite destination for diners.
